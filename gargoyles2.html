The gargoyles stop screaming and you and your friends successfully walk through the long corridor. At the end you find another door, this one appears to be unlocked. You walk around the room multiple times before you find an earring in the shape of a daisy. Wait, that is the same one Leonora was wearing the first night when you entered Hogwarts! You and your friends try every door in this new corridor, but you find nothing but empty classrooms. With a bad feeling in your stomach, you and your friends decide to go back to your common rooms. Maybe she will turn up fine the next day, you think to yourself that night in bed. Surely the professors would notice a missing student?
