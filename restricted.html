The Restricted Section

Phew, you escaped the gaze of Mrs. Norris and probably weeks of detention. You’re ready to turn back when… Wait, you realize you haven’t checked the restricted section yet. You convince your friends to turn around and look for Leonora in the restricted section. Luckily, when you get back into the library, Mrs. Norris is now fast asleep on a tower of books on the opposite side of the restricted section. You and your friends sneak towards the restricted section. When you get to the door of the restricted section, you notice that it’s locked with another one of those bloody muggle combination locks.
