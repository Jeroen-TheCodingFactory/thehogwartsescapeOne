Hogwarts Express

Congratulations! You just got your own wand, black walnut and dragon heartstring. They say that black walnut wood is less common than the standard walnut wand. The black walnut wand seeks a master of good instincts and powerful insight. This sounds very useful!

It’s been said that dragon heartstrings produce wands with the most power. Dragon wands tend to learn more quickly than other types. While they can change allegiance if won from their original master, they always bond strongly with the current owner. It is the most prone of the cores to accidents, being somewhat temperamental. Let’s hope this doesn’t cause you any trouble.

Now that you have your supplies it’s actually time to go to Hogwarts. Nervously you walk up to the brick wall that stands between platform 9 and 10. You walk faster and faster until it seems like you are about to crash into the brick wall in front of you. You close your eyes but the blow doesn’t come. Instead, you find yourself standing on the famous platform 9 ¾. A scarlet steam engine stands next to the platform and lets out puffs of smoke. The real Hogwarts Express, you can hardly believe it!
