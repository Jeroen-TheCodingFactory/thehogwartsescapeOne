Puzzle 12: Mrs. Norris

There are letters that will spell a word you need to get out of the situation. Maybe the symbols at the bottom can help you.

Well look at the squares in the note, don’t you think they look similar to the symbols at the bottom? Each symbol is part of a corner or middle and you take to the corresponding letter.

Merlin’s beard! Calm down. The answer here is ‘Avoid’. Just like how you’re avoiding your manners, gosh.
