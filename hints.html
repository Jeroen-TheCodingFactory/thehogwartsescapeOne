Hints

Welcome to the hints page of The Hogwarts Escape!

Are you stuck on that one bloody puzzle or can’t you seem to figure out what you have to do to complete the puzzle? You’re in the right place. You will find that all the hints for every puzzle are in chronological order. Simply click on the drop-down button for your first hint. The last drop-down button will always reveal the answer to the puzzle, but try to see what you can do with the hints first ;)
