The Great Lake

When you get off the train a giant of a man is waiting for you. His beard makes up almost his entire face but you can still see that he is smiling. He is holding a lantern and asks you to follow him. You follow a narrow path that leads to a black lake. At the end of this lake, you see a high mountain and atop is a giant castle; Hogwarts. A giant man points to the boats. You excitedly step into a boat with 3 others. A girl with dark curly hair, glasses and peculiar large daisy earrings sits down next to you.
