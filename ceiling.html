When you open the door one of you whispers about getting expelled if they find you here. You decide to ignore the comment and keep looking for your friend. When you reach the end of the restricted section you are about to give up hope until you hear a muffling sound that sounds somewhat like a Pixy. You look up to find the source of the sound and your mouth opens in shock, taped to the ceiling is your friend. “Leonora!” you say a little too loud, but at this moment you don’t care. “Guys we found her!” You look at the Spellotape that is keeping her body glued to the ceiling. How do you get her down?
