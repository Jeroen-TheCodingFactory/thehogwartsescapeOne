Tips

You're about to start your first year at Hogwarts, School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

The Hogwarts Escape is a combination of puzzles and storytelling. This consists of twenty-one puzzles and may be quite time-consuming. You can see the tips below on how to save your progress and read more useful information before starting your experience.


• We recommend playing this online escape room on either a desktop or laptop. It is not designed to be played on smartphones or tablets, however it is possible.
• You can play this online escape room by yourself or with friends. Just remember to keep the answers and puzzles to yourself or your group, so that many people may enjoy the escape room.
• This website doesn’t save the progress you’ve made. If you want to play the escape room over multiple days, you have to save the complete URL that you ended on.
• With some puzzles, you have to think harder than other puzzles. If you don’t know the answer to a puzzle you can always check our Hints page!
• Some puzzles can be solved more easily if you use pen and paper to write things down, or you could use a printer or something like Photoshop.
