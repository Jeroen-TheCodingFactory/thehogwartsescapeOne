The Hallway

Your first weeks at Hogwarts have been a great success! You and your friends have a lot of the same classes together. Leonora has become part of your friend group as well. You are happy to have a friend group that has people from all the different houses, because it makes for great friends and even better gossip.

After every breakfast, you meet up with your friends. However today is different, because where is Leonora? You look around the Great Hall but it's so busy that you can't make out if she's still sitting at the Slytherin table. You decide to look for her later in class but at the end of the day you and your friends still haven't seen her. Just as you're walking back from your last class you run into Peeves the Poltergeist. He turns to you and starts to rhyme:

"The girl is sad,
the girl is crying.
Hurry, hurry friends,
she could be dying"
