Puzzle 15: The Polyjuice Potion

You need to find a hidden word in the text. You’ve done this before I’m sure you can do it.

So there are numbers in front of every ingredient. Try to see if you can use those numbers in combination with the ingredients.

You’re quite persistent, aren’t you? You need to take every number and count to that amount of letters in the ingredient. For example: ‘1 Dried Leech’ = D.

The answer here is the spell ‘Diffindo’.
1 Dried leech
12 Boomslang skin pieces
9 Lacewing flies
1 Fine hair
16 grams of Crude Antimony
6 Bicorn Horns
8 Fluxweed roots
3 Knotgrass blades
