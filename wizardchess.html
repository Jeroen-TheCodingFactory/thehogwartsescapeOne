Wizard Chess

Could Leonora’s last name really be Travers? You want to ask her yourself, but you don’t want to upset her. You wait to see if she will talk to you about it, but she doesn’t.

It’s time for your friends to go home for Christmas break. You have decided to stay and make use of your time at Hogwarts. You know Serena’s last name is Bulstrode and now you (presumably) know Leonora’s real last name is Travers. You’ve heard about an old book in the library that contains information about Slytherin pureblood families and their history of the last 100 years. You want to get your hands on this book but according to the librarian a third years Slytherin has a hold of it. You trace down the guy who has the book in his possession and ask him if you can borrow it. He tells you that if you can beat him in a game of wizard chess he will allow you to borrow it from him. You accept the challenge, you’ve never lost a game of wizard chess after all. You decide to play with the black chess pieces.
