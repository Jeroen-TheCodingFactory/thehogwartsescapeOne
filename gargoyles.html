The Gargoyles

The door opens! But what a strange password.. You now enter a long, dark corridor with rows of gargoyles. They seem to be nothing more than statues, but when you and your friends take a step forward they start to scream. “Bloody hell, that’s loud!" A visible forcefield appears in front of you, blocking you and your friends from whatever is on the other side of the long corridor. You see Peeves fly through the forcefield while laughing maniacally, still singing the same four lines of rhyme.

You won’t let this stop you from getting to the other side of the hallway. You take a closer look at the statues. The first gargoyle on your right is creating a consistent gush of water, it looks so powerful that you don’t dare to step closer. The second gargoyle, across the room, is spewing a wall of fire across the hall. You have figured out that the third gargoyle is the one screaming loudly, which is creating the forcefield between you and the other side of the corridor. The fourth gargoyle, again to your right, is growing exponentially and nearly looks like he is going to burst through the ceiling any second now. What can you do to get past the gargoyles?
