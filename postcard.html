Slytherin Common Room

It appears that nobody has noticed the disappearance of Leonora, so you decide to take matters into your own hands. Luckily one of you is in the same house as Leonora. You hope to find answers in her dormitory. You walk to the Slytherin common room. As you walk through the room you see a few people sitting on the black leather couches near the crackling fireplace. You see two boys playing wizard chess against each other, one of them grinning and the other looking concentrated. "You're gonna lose this one, Carrow." the grinning boy says. You walk past them toward the girls dormitories. You decide to check Leonora’s belongings to see if there are any clues in there as to what might have happened.

Oh no! One of those muggle locks is attached to her trunk. Alohomora doesn’t seem to have any effect on the little numbered lock. It looks like you'll have to figure out the combination of the lock. You look around the room. On her nightstand you find a postcard addressed to Leonora from her mother. You turn it around and you see what you assume is her mother’s handwriting: You look at the front of the postcard and it just shows a picture of some potions.
