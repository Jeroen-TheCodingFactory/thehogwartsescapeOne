The Astronomy Tower

When the new semester starts, Leonora is nowhere to be found. She doesn’t show up to class at all the first week. That’s why you and your friends are surprised when she shows up in your Transfiguration class. You can’t wait to talk to her after class, but she is gone before you get the chance to.

You and your friends are on your way to the astronomy tower. You are studying the various moons of Jupiter in astronomy class. Tonight the lesson will only be focusing on one of Jupiter’s moon.
