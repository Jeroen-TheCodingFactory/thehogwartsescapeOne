The Harbour

When you get out in the underground harbour you meet up with your friends. The girl who was sitting next to you during the boat ride follows you. Your friends greet you and ask who the girl next to you is. Wait, you know she introduced herself to you but you were so distracted by everything around you that you already forgot.

What is the name of your new friend?
