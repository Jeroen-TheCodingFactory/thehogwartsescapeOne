The Final Exam

Detention. Two long months of it. You tell Leonora that it beats getting expelled but she isn’t so sure about that. You’re not completely sure why you are sharing the detention with the other two, though. Shouldn’t they have been expelled? You think to yourself. You look over at the other side of the classroom where Galen and Silas are writing lines quietly. They haven’t looked your way once. You don’t want to admit it, but you do feel bad for their families. But you definitely won’t admit that to Leonora.

The last months pass and you’ve nearly completed your first year at Hogwarts! But before you’ll be able to go to the second year in September, there’s something you have to do first. Pass your exam!

Don’t worry, though. You’ve had all year long to prepare and study. You’ll be fine.
