The Forbidden Forest

You know that there is no time to waste so you grab your wand and make a run for the stairs. The professor calls after you but the dread in your stomach keeps you from slowing down. You reach the Hogwarts grounds and you scan the treeline of the forest for Leonora. You hear faint shouting in the distance so you run towards the sound. When you reach the source you see the three people having a heated argument and you recognize the female voice immediately. You hide behind the nearest tree. Who are the two figures shouting at her?
